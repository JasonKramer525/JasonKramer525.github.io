93 MPH Club Scoreboard

The 93 MPH Club is Jomboy Media's competition they run weekly on Instagram. If you respond to one of their posts within the first 93 seconds, you get an entry into their weekly giveaway. Using a Web Scraper to scrape data from Instagram and with the help of a Python script, below are this week's statistics for the 93 MPH Club




View each week's standings below. Stats that are tracked are total posts (1 per post max), posts that are within the 93 second mark, best response time, worst response time, average response time and the closest miss to being within the 93 second mark.

If you commented multiple times on a post, then only your quickest response time on that post is counted.
